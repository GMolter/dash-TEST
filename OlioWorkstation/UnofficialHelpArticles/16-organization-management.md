# 🏢 Organization Management

The **Organization page** gives the organization Owner the tools to manage their team — controlling membership, access levels, and organization settings. Members have read-only access to this page.

Navigate here by opening the menu and selecting **Organization**.

---

## 📋 Overview Tab

The **Overview Tab** (visible to all members) shows:

- Organization **name**
- The 4-digit **invite code** with a one-click copy button
- A full **member list** with each member's display name, email, and role badge

Share the invite code with new teammates so they can join your organization.

---

## ✏️ Renaming the Organization

1. Go to **Organization → Manage Tab**
2. In the **Settings** section, click in the **Organization Name** field
3. Update the name
4. Click **Save Settings**

The new name appears immediately across the app (dashboard greeting, org page, etc.).

---

## 🔄 Regenerating the Invite Code

> ⚠️ **Warning:** Regenerating the invite code **immediately invalidates** the old code. Anyone who tries to join with the old code will receive an error. Make sure to share the new code with anyone who still needs to join.

To regenerate:

1. Go to **Organization → Manage Tab**
2. Click **Regenerate Code**
3. The new 4-digit code appears in a confirmation modal
4. Copy and share the new code

The old code is deactivated the moment you confirm.

---

## 👑 Promoting and Demoting Members

To change a member's role:

1. Go to **Organization → Manage Tab**
2. Find the member in the member list
3. Click **Promote** to raise them to a higher role
4. Click **Demote** to lower them back to Member

Changes take effect immediately. The affected member will have updated permissions on their next page load.

---

## ❌ Removing Members

To remove a member from your organization:

1. Go to **Organization → Manage Tab**
2. Find the member in the list
3. Click **Remove** next to their name
4. Confirm in the modal that appears

The removed member is immediately logged out of the organization context and returned to the organization setup screen. They must receive a new invite code to rejoin.

> ⚠️ **Warning:** Removing a member does not delete their personal data (personal pastes, personal Quick Links), but they lose access to all shared org resources.

## ⚠️ Important Limits

> ⚠️ **Warning:** You **cannot remove yourself** from the organization through the Manage tab. To leave, use the **Leave Organization** option in [Profile Settings](olio://help/profile-and-settings).

> ⚠️ **Warning:** The **Owner role cannot be transferred** through the UI. If you need to transfer ownership, contact your system administrator.
